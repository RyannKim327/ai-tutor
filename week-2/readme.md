# Identifying Patterns in Numbers

Welcome to Week 2! This week, we will explore the concept of patterns using sequences of numbers. Patterns are all around us, from nature to music, and recognizing them can help you understand how things work.

## Understanding Patterns
Think of patterns like rhythms in music or steps in a dance. Just like you might tap your feet in a consistent way to a song, numbers can change in a consistent way too. For example:
- If you see the numbers 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, ... you can recognize that they all increase by 1 every time. This is a simple pattern.
- If you see the numbers 10, 8, 6, 4, ... you can see that they decrease by 2. This is another kind of pattern.

### How to Approach This Challenge
1. **Observe**: Look at the given sequence of numbers carefully.
2. **Identify**: Try to find out how the numbers are changing. Are they going up or down? By how much?
3. **Conclusion**: Write down what pattern you notice, if any.

### Moving Forward

Then, figure out how they relate to each other—do they increase, decrease, or follow some other pattern? This process will help you think logically and develop structured reasoning skills.
